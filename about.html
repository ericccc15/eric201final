ABOUT ME

THIS IS ME AND MY FAMILY

I am Eric Chen. I am currently a freshman at Brigham Young University studying Accounting. I was born and raised in Taiwan my whole life. I used to attend local school, where everything was taught in Mandarin and there was only an English class once a week. My life was changed when I got an opportunity to continue my education at BYU-Idaho. The experience allowed me to meet people from different countries and I was doing everything in English rather Mandarin. It was a huge transition for me, but little did I know there was a bigger one to come. During the time when I was studying at BYUI, I figured out I have a strong passion for business. After taking different business classes, reached out to professors, alumni, I decided to pursue investment banking as my future career since I cannot think of other industry can provide as much value as investment banking would. Therefore, I decided to transfer to BYU in Provo, UT, because BYU offer better resources and education in business, and I thought it will be helpful for me to break into investment banking. It was by far the biggest transition in my life, but it was honestly the best decision I have ever made. I have been such a blessing to be here! The people here are truly one of a kind. Although sometimes it is hard being in a foreign country alone far from family and home, I’m thankful for this opportunity, this university, the people I’ve met here, my professors, and God. This is the best decision I ever made in my life, and I cannot wait to experience this wonderful opportunity.
